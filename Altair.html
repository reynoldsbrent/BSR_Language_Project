Why Significant

The signifigance of the Altair Computer was it was the first commercially succesful computer of its time in the 1970s. This computer played a huge role in the microcomputer history inspiring companies as microsoft and apple. It was known as the first personal computer which was huge step for technology in its time. What made it so big was it was affordable to the general public not just wealthy people.

History

The Altair 8800 was invented by Ed Roberts in 1975, it sold for 200 to 400 dollars during its time. The main reason Roberts built this machine was to save his company in all honesty. Many people used this machine because it was affordable and easy to figure out. It was one of the first succesful personal computer. The machine itself had intricate front panels with switches and led lights which allowed consumers to interact with the computer directly. This machine paved the way for the development of modern computer history. The impact this machine had still goes noticed today, it spawned great growth within the computer markets. The computer had 256 bytes of memory.
